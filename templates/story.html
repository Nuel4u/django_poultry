our story

Finity Farm Ltd. is proud to supply Newfoundland and Labrador with quality, made-right-here, fresh and individually fresh frozen chicken products.

Since being established, we’ve developed into this province’s only fully-integrated chicken producer and processor. That means we’re involved in every stage of the process – we know where our product is going, and where it’s been, all the way from farm to plate.

From our modern barns, with plenty of space, and 24-hour-a-day access to fresh feed and clean water, federally-inspected processing where we employ hundreds of Newfoundlanders, Country Ribbon always goes the distance to ensure quality and safety – after all, we live and work right here in your community.
