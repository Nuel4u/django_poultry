Farm Labourers (Part-time)

Finity Farm Ltd

FARM LABOURERS (Part-time)

Finity Farm Ltd. is an integrated livestock production and processing company that markets products under the Country Ribbon, Pinehill and other brand names. Country Ribbon operates a livestock feed mill, production complex and processing plant in the St. John’s area.

We have openings at our Cochrane Pond (St. John’s area) Poultry Farm Complex for Farm Labourers. Applicants must be willing to work in a farm setting with live animals and be mechanically inclined, have own transportation and be willing to work shift work. This is a unionized position with a starting rate of pay of $18.50 per hour. Possession of a valid driver’s license is required. Previous experience in working in an agricultural environment would be a definite asset.

Resumes may be either:

  • Mailed to P.O. Box 803, St. John’s, NL A1C 5L7
  • Faxe to 709-722-6010
  • Emailed to careers@countryribbon.com
  • Please indicate FARM LABOURER on the envelope or fax sheet.
